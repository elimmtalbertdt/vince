2021

>

Good Smile Racing's (GSR) 2021 campaign featured a distinctive Racing Miku design by En Morikura. The team, with their Mercedes-AMG GT3, showed competitive pace throughout the season, consistently finishing in the top-10. A notable performance was their 5th place finish at Round 4 Motegi. This steady form contributed to them securing 34 points, earning them a 10th place finish in the driver standings for the season.
