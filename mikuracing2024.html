Good Smile Racing (GSR) continued their Hatsune Miku GT Project in the 2024 SUPER GT GT300 class. The team's Mercedes-AMG GT3 Evo, driven by Nobuteru Taniguchi and Tatsuya Kataoka, featured a new Racing Miku design by Mogumo. Highlights include a 4th place finish at Round 2 Fuji and a 2nd place at Round 4 Fuji. They finished the season 8th in the driver points standings with 30 points.
