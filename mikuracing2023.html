2023

Good Smile Racing's (GSR) 2023 season with their Mercedes-AMG GT3 featured a Racing Miku design by Toridamono. A highlight was securing pole position at Round 4 Fuji, demonstrating their qualifying prowess. However, converting this into consistent race finishes proved challenging. The team concluded the season with 3 points, placing them 19th in the driver standings. Despite flashes of speed, overall race results didn't reflect their qualifying potential.
